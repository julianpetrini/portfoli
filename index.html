About me

just a little bit

I am Julian, a dual Swiss and Argentine citizen. From a young age, I have been captivated by the world of ideas and creativity. Although I spent my entire life in Buenos Aires, Argentina, I made the bold decision to pursue my dreams and move to Switzerland to better understand my roots and become the best version of myself. I am sure you have experienced a similar moment of exhilaration, as I did in August 2021 when I arrived in Zurich..


/ BRIEF PATH

this is me

In January of last year, I enrolled in the comprehensive web developer program, Opportunity Zurich , which spanned 1023 hours and covered a range of skills including Figma, HTML5, CSS3, Sass, Bootstrap, Javascript, Vue js, CMS, MySQL, Laravel, GIT, and Github. The program culminated in September with my final project, Dreamcloud .
Currently, I am seeking my first web developer role in Switzerland while learning German and completing a Google UX Design course . Throughout my career, I have developed a diverse skill set by working in a variety of roles such as content creator, customer service agent, salesman, and more. I have honed my social skills and have consistently added value in these positions. I have also worked for both large companies such as Citibank, GE, and the Leo Messi Foundation, as well as local stores and freelance projects.
My journey began as a video producer, where I not only produced but also sold my ideas and projects. Over time, I have gained experience in design, content creation, and team management, always adapting and learning new tools. However, I felt something was missing and one day, I discovered web development and fell in love with its limitless potential for creation and continuous learning. This field allows me to connect with the world and make a real impact.
