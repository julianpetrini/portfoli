About me

just a little bit

I'm Julian. Im a swiss and argentinean citizen. I'm in love with the world of ideas and creativity since I can remember. / BRIEF PATH

this is me

Last January I was able to enter Opportunity Zurich . A complete web developer's program of 1023 hours including: Figma, HTML5, CSS3, Sass, Bootstrap, Javascript, Vue js, CMS, MySQL, Laravel, GIT, Github. This ended on September with my final project Dreamcloud.
Nowadays looking for my first experience as a web developer in Switzerland while studying German and doing a Google UX Design course .
All my past career was about being a content creator, a customer service agent, a salesman, and many more useful experiences. Furthermore, I was always combining social skills and delivering value. I've managed to work in big companies such as Citibank, GE, Leo Messi foundation, and also in local stores and freelance projects.
I started as a video producer while working in sales. This helped me not only to be the one who produces but also the one who sells the idea or project. Throughout this time I've designed logos, created content, and organized teams depending on the needs of the client and the project. While this happened I was always learning new tools. But I felt something was missing. One day I entered the developer field and fell in love right away. A limitless opportunity to create and learn while staying connected to the world we live in.
